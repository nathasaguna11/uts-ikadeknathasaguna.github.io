Welcome to Tegenungan Waterfall


Tegenungan Waterfall is a waterfall located in Kemenuh Village, Sukawati District, Gianyar Regency, is 30 km from Denpasar City. This waterfall has a height of 15 meters. Although not so high, the water discharge is very heavy. The water is also clean and clear. This waterfall originates from the Tukad Petanu River. The place it is often used to do extreme things, such as jumping from the top of a waterfall.
